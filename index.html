FAQ

Quick answers for your subscribers.

How do I install the EA?

After purchase, download the .ex5 from your dashboard and place it in your MT5 Experts folder. Then restart MT5 and attach it to a chart.

How do I bind my account?

In your dashboard, enter your MT5 Account # and your Nickname (case-sensitive). The EA input Nickname must match exactly.

My prop firm changed my MT5 account number ‚Äî what do I do?

Go to your dashboard and replace your MT5 Account # and/or Nickname. Changes are unlimited, but only 1 active account can be bound at a time.

What happens if my payment fails?

Your license becomes inactive immediately (soft lock): the EA stays on chart and can manage/close existing trades, but will not open new trades.

Do you offer refunds?

No. All sales are final.
