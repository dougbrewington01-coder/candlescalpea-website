FAQ

How do I install the EA?

Download your .ex5 after payment, then place it into MT5 Experts folder and restart MT5.

How do I bind my account?

Enter your MT5 account number + nickname on the website, then type the same nickname in the EA inputs.

My prop firm changed my MT5 account number — what now?

Remove the old bind and add the new MT5 account number + nickname. Unlimited changes.

What happens if payment fails?

Your license is soft-locked immediately: no new trades; existing trades can be managed/closed safely.
