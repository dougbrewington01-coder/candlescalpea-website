CandleScalpEA 5.0 (MT5)

CandleScalpEA 5.0 is delivered through a monthly subscription. To protect subscribers, access requires verified email and an active subscription.

⚠️ Make sure you are using a MetaTrader 5 account when signing up.

Important: Each subscription is for one active MT5 account at a time. If your prop firm gives you a new MT5 account number after passing a challenge, you can remove the old account number and add the new one. If you want to use the EA on multiple accounts, you’ll need to purchase an additional subscription.

Features

Email verification required

You must verify your email before purchasing a subscription or accessing downloads.

License tied to MT5 + Nickname

Your access is validated using your MT5 account number + your nickname (case-sensitive).

Prop firm account number changed? Remove the old number and add the new one anytime.

How It Works

1) Create your account

Sign up with email + password, then verify your email to unlock access.

2) Subscribe

Purchase the $50/month subscription to unlock your download and license access.

3) Add your MT5 account number + nickname

Enter your MT5 account number and the nickname you want to use (nickname is case-sensitive).

4) Download + install

Download the EA and install it in MT5. Then type your nickname into the EA inputs exactly as saved on your account.

FAQ

Where do I type my nickname in MT5?

In MT5, add the EA to your chart, open the EA Inputs tab, and type your nickname exactly (case-sensitive).

My prop firm gave me a new MT5 account number. Can I update it?

Yes. Remove your old account number on your dashboard and add the new one. Changes are allowed anytime.

How many accounts can I use per subscription?

One active MT5 account at a time per subscription. If you want multiple accounts, you’ll need multiple subscriptions.

What happens if my payment fails?

Your access is soft-locked immediately: the EA will not open new trades, but it can still manage/close existing trades safely.
